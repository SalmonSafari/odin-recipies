Yummy lasagna

Description

this is a Lasagna recipie, that has a great taste and ready to become your next classic!

Try this easy to make lasagna today!

Ingredients (2 portions):

  • 1 yellow onion
  • 1 colve of garlic
  • 250 grams of groundbeef
  • 1/2 tablespoon cooking oil
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 250 grams crushed tomatos
  • 1/2 meat broth
  • salt
  • peper
  • 4 1/2 dried lasagne slabs

sauce

  • 90g food fat
  • 3 tablespoons flour
  • 5dl milk
  • 1dl parmesan

Steps:

  1. put the oven on 175c
  2. Peel and chop the onion and garlic. Saut√© minced meat, onion and garlic in the oil in a frying pan. Add tomato puree that is allowed to sizzle with the mince. Season with thyme and rosemary. Add crushed tomatoes and stock cube, let it boil for about 10 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Sauce: Melt the shortening and stir in the flour. Dilute with the milk while whisking. Let the sauce boil for about 5 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. layer the sauce, lasagne plates and the minced meat sauce in an oven-safe form (approx. 20 x 30 cm). Finish with sauce and parmesan cheese
  5. put the Lasagna in the oven for 40 minutes and wait
  6. For plating: Eat the lasgna with salad of choice, and/or cheese!
